Sliding Doors & Windows

Sliding Doors & Windows are the perfect solution for modern homes that need to save space and create a seamless connection between indoor and outdoor living areas. With their sleek and simple design, they’re ideal for living rooms, balconies, and patios.

Please contact us

Key Features & Benefits

  • Space-Saving: The sliding track system eliminates the need for swing space, ideal for tight rooms.
  • Easy to Operate: High-quality rollers ensure smooth and effortless operation.
  • Expansive Views: Large glass panels allow abundant light and a panoramic view.
  • Sleek & Modern: Clean lines create a minimalist aesthetic that fits any modern design.

Considerations

  • Limited Ventilation: Only half the total area can open, reducing airflow.
  • Track Maintenance: The bottom track needs regular cleaning for smooth movement.

Usage & Application Guide

  • Patio Doors: Create a seamless flow between living spaces and the outdoors.
  • Room Dividers: Great for separating spaces while keeping light and openness.
  • Large Windows: Perfect for maximizing natural light and wide outdoor views.
