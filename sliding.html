Sliding Doors & Windows

Sliding Doors & Windows aare the perfect solution for modern homes that need to save space and create a seamless connection between indoor and outdoor living areas. With their sleek and simple design, they are an ideal choice for living rooms, balconies and patios.

Please contact us

Key Features & Benefits

  • Space-Saving: Since the door and window sashes slide along a track, they do not require extra space to open. This makes them ideal for tight spaces.
  • Easy to Operate: Our high-quality rollers and tracks ensure smooth and effortless operation, making them easy for anyone to use.
  • Expansive Views: The large glass panels provide a wide, unobstructed view and allow for abundant natural light to fill your home.
  • Sleek, Modern Look: A sliding glass door offers a clean, contemporary aesthetic that can instantly modernize any space.

Considerations

  • Limited Ventilation: You can only open about half of the total area, which may limit airflow compared to casement or folding doors.
  • Track Maintenance: The bottom track can accumulate dirt and debris, requiring regular cleaning to ensure smooth operation.

Usage & Application Guide

  • Patio Doors: They are the most common choice for creating an easy flow between your indoor living room and outdoor patio or deck.
  • Room Dividers: Can be used as a stylish and space-saving room divider between interior spaces, such as a living room and dining area.
  • Large Windows: Ideal for rooms that benefit from a great view or ample natural light, such as bedrooms or home offices.
