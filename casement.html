Casement Windows & Swing Doors

Casement windows and swing doors are timeless designs that remain incredibly popular. Their simple, durable mechanism allows your home to benefit from abundant natural ventilation and a bright, open atmosphere.

Please contact us

Key Features & Benefits

  • Superior Ventilation: Opens up to 90°, allowing excellent airflow and cross-ventilation.
  • Enhanced Security: Multi-point locking system secures the sash tightly to the frame.
  • Energy Efficiency: Tight seal provides insulation, reducing energy bills.
  • Easy Maintenance: Full-opening design allows safe cleaning from inside.

Considerations

  • Space Requirement: Sash swings outward and requires clearance space.
  • Size Limitations: Casement sashes are smaller, limiting glass area.

Usage & Application Guide

  • Casement Windows: Ideal for kitchens and bathrooms requiring airflow.
  • Swing Doors: Perfect for balconies, gardens, or main entrances — secure and elegant.
