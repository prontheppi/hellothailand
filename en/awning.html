Awning Windows

Awning windows are designed to open outwards from the bottom. This unique design allows continuous airflow even on rainy days, providing superior ventilation while maintaining privacy.

Key Features & Benefits

  • Rain Protection: The outward-opening design acts like an awning, allowing airflow even during rain.
  • Continuous Airflow: Provides passive ventilation throughout the day.
  • Privacy and Security: Ideal for bathrooms and basements, ensuring light and privacy.
  • Energy Efficiency: Airtight seals improve insulation and reduce energy loss.
