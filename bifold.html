Folding/Bi-fold Doors & Windows

Folding or bi-fold doors and windows are the ultimate design for creating a seamless connection between your indoor and outdoor spaces. With their simple and modern folding mechanism, they allow you to open up an entire wall, creating a spacious and luxurious feel.

Please contact us

Key Features & Benefits

  • Full Opening: The folding mechanism allows the entire system to be stacked neatly to one side, providing a complete opening and an unobstructed passage.
  • Aesthetic Appeal: Folding doors create a bold and luxurious statement, elevating the overall look of your home.
  • Panoramic Views: The large glass panels offer a stunning, panoramic view of your surroundings.
  • Weather Resistant: Our bi-fold doors come with a robust sealing system that provides superior protection against rain and wind.

Considerations

  • Stacking Space: When fully opened, the folded panels require space to stack on one or both sides, which needs to be considered during the design phase.
  • Higher Cost: Due to the complex mechanism and larger size, they are generally more expensive than other door and window types.

Usage & Application Guide

  • Indoor-Outdoor Flow: Idel for connecting living rooms to gardens, pools, or patios, creating a seamless transition.
  • Commercial Spaces: A great choice for restaurants and cafes that want to create an open and inviting atmosphere.
  • Entertaining Areas: Perfect for large entertaining spaces where you want to provide a sense of luxury and grand scale.
