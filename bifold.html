Casement Windows & Swing Doors

Casement windows and swing doors are timeless designs that remain incredibly popular. With their simple yet durable mechanism, they allow your home to benefit from abundant natural ventilation and create a bright, open atmosphere.

Please contact us

Key Features & Benefits

  • Superior Ventilation: Casement windows open to a full 90 degrees, allowing for excellent airflow and effective cross-ventilation within your home.
  • Enhanced Security: When fully closed, the multi-point locking system secures the sash tightly to the frame, providing exceptional security against forced entry.
  • Energy Efficiency: The tight seal between the sash and frame provides superior insulation, helping to keep your home cool and reducing your energy bills.
  • Easy to Maintain: The full-opening design makes it simple and safe to clean both the inside and outside of the window from within your home.

Considerations

  • Space Requirement: Since the sash swings outward, it requires clearance space. This may not be ideal for homes with narrow walkways or limited exterior space.
  • Size Limitations: To maintain stability, casement window sashes are generally smaller compared to other window types, which can limit the overall glass area.

Usage & Application Guide

  • Casement Windows: An excellent choice for kitchens and bathrooms that need good ventilation, as well as living rooms where you want to maximize natural light and airflow.
  • Swing Doors: Ideal for front entryways, balcony doors, or doors leading to a garden, providing a sophisticated and secure entrance.
