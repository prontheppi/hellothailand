Hanging Doors

Hanging doors or sliding barn doors are a popular design choice that saves space while adding a striking feature to any room. With their top-mounted track system, they slide along the wall, eliminating the need for swing clearance — a perfect fit for modern and minimalist homes.

Please contact us

Key Features & Benefits

  • Ultimate Space Saver: Barn doors don't require swing space — ideal for tight corners or small rooms.
  • Easy Installation: Top-mounted hardware makes installation simple and stylish.
  • Unique Style: Available in modern, rustic, or traditional designs to match your interior.
  • Smooth Operation: High-quality rollers ensure quiet and smooth sliding motion.

Considerations

  • Limited Sound & Odor Proofing: Hanging doors don't fully seal, reducing sound and odor insulation.
  • No Privacy Lock: Some models lack locks, making them less suitable for private rooms.

Usage & Application Guide

  • Room Dividers: Stylishly separates living spaces like kitchens or bedrooms.
  • Open-Concept Bathrooms: Adds a contemporary touch to en-suite bathrooms.
  • Closets and Pantries: Perfect for compact storage areas, offering easy access and sleek aesthetics.
