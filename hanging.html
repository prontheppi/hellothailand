Awning Windows

Hanging doors or sliding barn doors are a popular design choice that saves space while adding a striking feature to any room. With Their top-mounted track system, they slide along the wall, elminating the need for swing clearance and making them a perfect solution for modern and minimalist homes.

Please contact us

Key Features & Benefits

  • Ultimate Space Saver: Barn Doors do not require a swinging radius, making them an excellent choice for small rooms, tight corners, or narrow hallways.
  • Easy Installation: Their top-mounted hardware system makes them relatively easy to install and they can even be a statement piece in your interior design.
  • Unique Style: Available in a variety of styles, from modern and sleek to rustic and traditional, allowing them to perfectly complement your home's aesthetic.
  • Smooth Operation: The high-quality rollers ensure a smooth and quiet sliding motion, providing a hassle-free experience.

Considerations

  • Limited Sound & Odor Proofing: Since hanging doors do not seal completely, they are not as effective at blocking sound and odor as traditional doors.
  • No Privacy Lock: Some models do not come with a locking mechanism, making them unsuitable for rooms that require a high level of privacy.

Usage & Application Guide

  • Room Dividers: An excellent way to stylishly separate a living room from a kitchen or a bedroom from a walk-in closet.
  • Open-Concept Bathrooms: Can be used as a striking door for an en-suite bathroom to add a modern touch.
  • Closets and Pantries: Perfect for pantries or closets, where they provide easy access and a contemporary look without the need for a traditional swinging door.
